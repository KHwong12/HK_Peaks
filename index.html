Peaks in Hong Kong

Where and How Much Can You See?

With numerous amount of hills and peaks in Hong Kong, have you ever investigated the visable area from the peaks? This application shows the peaks and hills in Hong Kong. You could see where and how much you could see from the peaks with given visible distance.

NOTE: This application is still in development.

How to Use

  1. Drag and wander around the 3D scene and investigate the hills.
  2. Click on the peaks (or any other places you like). The application will compute the visible area/viewshed (the white grids) from the location you clicked.
  3. Drag the slider to change the visibility from the peaks.
