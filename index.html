Peaks in Hong Kong

Where and How Much Can You See?

NOTE: This application is still in development.

With numerous amount of hills and peaks in Hong Kong, have you ever investigated the visable area from the peaks? This application allows you to view and check where and how much you could see from the peaks with given visible distance.

In this 3d map, the peaks are all labelled with their Chinese and English name, as well as peak elevation.

How to Use

  1. Drag and wander around the 3D scene and investigate the topography of Hong Kong.
  2. Click on the peaks (or any other places you like). The application will compute the visible area (in jargon, viewshed) from that point.
  3. The white grids appears on the map shows the area visible from the centre.
  4. Drag the slider to set your desired visibility (1-20km) to simulate how far you can see from the centre.

Current Visibility

Current visibility data are provided by Hong Kong Observatory.
