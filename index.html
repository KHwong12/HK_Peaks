Peaks in Hong Kong

Where and How Much Can You See?

There are many hills in Hong Kong. Where (and what) could you see from the peak? This application shows the peaks and hills in Hong Kong and where you could see from the peaks with given visible distance.

How to Use

  • Drag and wander around the 3D scene and investigate the hills.
  • Click on the peaks (or any other places you like) to check the area visible (the white grids) from the peak.
  • Drag the slider to change the visibility from the peaks..
